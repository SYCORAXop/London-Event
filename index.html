About the event

India's Most Prestigious Property Show,
Now in London

The Indian Property Show is a globally touring, free-to-attend real estate exhibition that connects High-Net-Worth Non-Resident Indians directly with India's most reputable developers, bypassing agents, aggregators and hidden fees entirely.

"From Germany and USA to now London, we bring India's finest investment opportunities directly to the London NRI community."

Each edition is a curated, intimate experience. Developers present exclusive pre-launch inventory and investment structures directly to qualified NRI buyers, in one room, over one focused weekend.
